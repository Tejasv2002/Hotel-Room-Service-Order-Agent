# Example Conversations (short, realistic flows)

## 1) Clear vegetarian order
Guest: "I'd like a Caesar salad, please. I'm vegetarian."
Agent: "Order confirmed: Caesar Salad"

## 2) Ambiguous name -> clarification
Guest: "Can I have a sandwich?"
Agent: "I found multiple items that match: Grilled Chicken Sandwich, Club Sandwich. Which one would you like?"
Guest: "The club sandwich."
Agent: "Order confirmed: Club Sandwich"

## 3) Dietary conflict and alternative suggestion
Guest: "I want something vegan. How about pancakes?"
Agent: "Note: this item may conflict with preferences: vegan. Would you like me to suggest alternatives that fit your preferences?"
Guest: "Yes, suggest."
Agent: "Vegan Buddha Bowl is available. Order confirmed: Vegan Buddha Bowl"

## 4) Item unavailable -> alternatives
Guest: "I'd like Peanut Butter Cookies."
Agent: "Sorry, Peanut Butter Cookies is currently unavailable. May I suggest: [other similar items]?"
